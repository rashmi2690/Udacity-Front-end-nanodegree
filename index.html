Rashmi Rajshekhar

Hey there! I am a twenty eight year old engineer living in NYC. I am a biomedical engineer, by trade, and an aspiring writer and poet.

I got my Bachelors degree from Manipal Institute of Technology, India followed by my Masters degree at Cornell University, both in Biomedical Engineering. After Cornell, I finally found my passion project, at the intersection of health and technology. I worked for three years as a full time research assistant in the Hood Visual Science Lab at Columbia University, under the guidance of Dr. Donald C. Hood.
